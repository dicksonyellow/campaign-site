My Plans

We will create a system of empowerment that will enable young skilled Nigerians to become economically viable.

We will create a talent pool of Nigerians to foster career opportunities and then liaise with HR and head hunters to recruit directly from our database.

We will provide the platform for Nigerian business owners to take advantage of existing and emerging business opportunities as well as funding for start-ups and current businesses.

We will create initiatives, projects, and activities to promote and engage Family life for Nigerian families and Polish Nigerian families.

We will create a legal system and structure to advocate and defend the rights of Nigerians in Poland as well as residency issues.

We will create a welfare and support system for all Nigerians who are in need.

We will facilitate educational sports, social-cultural business, and economic conferences that will benefit Nigerians in Poland.

We will ensure that Nigerians in Poland have ease of Passport renewal.
